About Us

Tayloredbypresh was born out of a passion for fashion, faith, and the fierce beauty of women in all shapes and sizes. Founded in Nigeria, our brand is dedicated to designing clothing that does more than just fit — it transforms.

Every piece is created with precision, from fabric selection to the final stitch, to help women feel confident, sexy, and powerful. We are more than a fashion brand — we are a movement.

At the heart of our designs lies resilience, excellence, and strong faith. We believe that every woman deserves to be seen, celebrated, and styled in a way that reflects her inner strength. That’s why we cater to all sizes and create styles that make every woman feel bold and beautiful. As we grow, our mission is to expand across borders, build fashion houses, and create job opportunities for women across Nigeria and beyond — using fashion as a tool to uplift and empower. With Tayloredbypresh, you're not just wearing fashion — you're wearing purpose.

Preshy, CEO
